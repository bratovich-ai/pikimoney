Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from the Budget Tracking App, please follow the instructions below.

Steps to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to using the subject line: "Delete My Account - PikiMoney".
  2. Include your registered email address or username in the message body.
  3. Your data will be deleted within 7 business days, and you will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your account credentials (email, username, password)
  • Your personal budget entries and reports
  • Chat history with the AI assistant (if stored)

Data Retention Policy

We do not retain any data after account deletion. Some anonymized analytics or crash logs may remain for statistical purposes but are not linked to your identity.
